Almost all coconut oils available in our country are made from copra that has been smoked with sulphur. This is a very common practice across India. Sulphur smoking is done to ensure a higher shelf life for the copra while storing it. In India, coconut oil is mostly used for hair & skin application, but coconut oil made from sulphur smoked copra is not ideal for cooking. We at KLF Coconad ensure our coconut cooking oil is made from copra that is not smoked with sulphur at any stage of the process. Enter KLF, a name synonymous with innovation & quality in the coconut industry. With decades of experience, KLF has always been at the forefront of revolutionising the coconut-based product market.
